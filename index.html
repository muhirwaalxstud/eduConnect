About EduConnect Rwanda

Welcome to EduConnect Rwanda - your partner in transformative education! We are dedicated to revolutionizing the educational landscape in Rwanda by bridging the gap between academic knowledge and real-world skills.

Our mission is to provide comprehensive services that empower students to succeed in their careers. Whether it's through our career fair guidance, inspirational talks, mentorship programs, workshops, boot camps, or meet-ups, EduConnect Rwanda is committed to nurturing a skilled and motivated workforce for the future.

Founded by university students Dukundane Stephen and Muhirwa Jean Bosco, EduConnect Rwanda envisions a future where every graduate not only excels academically but is also well-prepared for the dynamic challenges of the workforce.
